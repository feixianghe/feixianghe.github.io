I am Feixiang He(贺飞翔), an Assistant Professor at the School of Electronic Information, Central South University. I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Leeds in Oct 2023 and subsequently conducted postdoctoral research at the Virtual Environment and Computer Graphics (VECG) group at University College London(UCL) from Mar to Oct 2024, under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. He Wang.

Before that, I received my Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees from Sichuan University in 2014 and 2017, respectively, advised by Prof. Zhisheng You and Prof. Qijun Zhao. From February 2017 to August 2018, I worked as a Research Assistant at Nanyang Technological University(NTU).
